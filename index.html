This is the Blob Mentality Website!

This is for group, Fantasy 9's CSS385 Final Project game: Blob Mentality

Blob Mentality is about a world in a standard RPG setting, think Final Fantasy, D&D, or more relatably: Dragon Quest, with their iconic Slimes. In this game you take on the role of a lowly Slime and your goal is to consume everybody in the castle! Use your captives to increase in size or sell them to the Necromancer to upgrade your abilities to combat the increasing odds against you.
